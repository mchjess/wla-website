Braidwood

Exploring my understanding of 'spirit of place' through landscape.

These digital photographs form the start of my upcoming solo exhibition at the Braidwood Regional Arts Gallery. The images have been taken at sunset at local spots around town. They will form part of a larger collection of works featuring printmaking, acyrlic and oil painting.
